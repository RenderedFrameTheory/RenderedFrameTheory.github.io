Feedback & “Chat” (Asynchronous)

This is the main channel for suggestions, questions, and technical discussion. Think of it as a slow, permanent chat log.

Send a message

Your message will open in your email client addressed to the RFT inbox. Replies come back to the email you provide.

How this “chat” works

  • You write a message here; your email client opens with everything pre-filled.
  • You send the email; it lands in the RFT inbox as a thread.
  • Replies keep the subject line, so each topic becomes a long-term “chat”.
  • No external platform, no logins — just email threading.

Partnerships & Deals

For serious offers only: research collaborations, funding, licensing, hosted compute, or media work tied to Rendered Frame Theory.

Submit a proposal

Partnership criteria

  • Aligned with RFT’s core: truth-first, no gatekeeping, no IP theft.
  • Respect for existing Zenodo DOIs, SHA hashes, and legal protections.
  • Clear benefit for the development or validation of Rendered Frame Theory.
  • No interest in “black box” deals that bury data or results.

For large-scale proposals, you can also request an NDA-based channel in your initial email.
