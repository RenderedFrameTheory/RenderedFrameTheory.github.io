Ground rules
Simple, non-negotiable rules so people can share serious work without noise.
  • 1. Respect. Critique ideas, not people. No abuse, no harassment, no slurs.
  • 2. Be specific. Every submission must state clearly what is being claimed or tested.
  • 3. Show your working. Equations, methods, code or data links where possible. Hand-waving is not enough.
  • 4. Own your material. Only upload or link work you have the right to share. You retain your rights; this page is a hub.
  • 5. No spam. Repeated low-effort promotion or irrelevant links will be removed.
